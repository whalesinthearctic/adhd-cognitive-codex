Sample Patterns

A taste of what you'll get

Each of the 64 patterns includes what it is, why it happens, and the specific correction to fix it.

Doom Scrolling

Each new piece of content provides a micro-hit of dopamine. Your brain found an infinite drip.

Correction: App timers (15 min max). Delete apps from home screen. Replace with Dopamine Menu activities.

Task Initiation Failure

Knowing exactly what to do but being unable to start. Your prefrontal cortex can't fire the 'start' signal.

Correction: The 2-minute start: commit to only 2 minutes. Lower the bar until starting feels trivial.

Rejection Sensitivity Dysphoria

Perceiving rejection or ambiguous signals as devastating attacks. A tone change triggers a full crisis.

Correction: Label it: 'This is RSD, not reality.' Rate evidence 1-10. Wait 2 hours before responding.

Time Blindness

Two time zones: NOW and NOT NOW. Anything not happening now doesn't exist until it suddenly becomes NOW.

Correction: Visual countdowns, physical calendars, progressive alarms. Make the future visible.

Shame Accumulation

Every past failure adds a 'brick' to the emotional wall between you and future tasks.

Correction: Name the shame. Separate past from present capability.

Boom-Bust Productivity

Superhuman output followed by complete collapse. 14-hour sprints then 3-day crashes.

Correction: Cap productive days at 6-8 hours even when unstoppable. Sustainable output wins.
